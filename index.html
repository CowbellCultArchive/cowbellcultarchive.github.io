Cowbell
Cult

A phonk collective formed online in 2020, built around pitched 808 cowbells and a steady run of volume tapes in the Memphis phonk tradition.

Phonk Collective Est. Oct 12, 2020 Disbanded 2024
[ 01 ]

The Sound

The group built melodies out of percussion: 808 cowbells mapped across different pitches, with long-decay 808 kicks tuned into basslines. Records were made on inexpensive drum machines, samplers and cassette recorders.

They released first on SoundCloud and VK before reaching streaming platforms. The best-known tracks are JOEHDAH — "Smoke" (Vol. 1), JOEHDAH — COP KILLAZ (Vol. 5) and $werve — CITY LIGHT$.

[ 02 ]

The Roster

Founded by pilgrim (a.k.a. Grimane). The original lineup was C4, JOEHDAH, Joetor 6 (later Remymane), G Pimpin (DOM1N1C), SilvaroundS, DIRSA, PUMPMANE and UZIMANE. The wider roster grew to more than thirty affiliated producers and DJs before the collective disbanded.
